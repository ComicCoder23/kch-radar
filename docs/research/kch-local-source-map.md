- **East Dunbartonshire Council News**: Public initiatives and news. Trust: High. Freshness: High. Rule: Link-only.
- **East Dunbartonshire Asset Map**: Community organisations and places. Trust: Medium-High. Freshness: Medium. Rule: Link-only / Open data.

- **British Newspaper Archive**: Local press coverage. Trust: High. Freshness: Static. Rule: Subscription/Access-based.

## Verification Notes
- Sources categorized as "Link-only" should generally be presented as curated discovery routes to maintain provenance and trust.
- "Request-based" sources require manual outreach or on-site engagement (e.g., EDLC archives).
- High-trust/High-freshness sources should form the automated ingest stack for KCH.
- All manually discovered listings must undergo verification before surfacing in KCH as active nodes.
